Services Offered:

- Life Skills and Social Integration

Our tailored programs are designed to equip individuals with daily living skills and enhance their social interactions. We aim to empower them to lead independent and fulfilling lives.

- Hygiene and Self-Care Support

Confidence and well-being start with personal hygiene and self-care. Our dedicated team provides guidance in developing these essential habits, ensuring our clients are well-prepared for their daily challenges.

- Recreation Activities

We believe in the power of play and physical activity. With the sportive skill programs, it is aimed to develop fine motor, gross motor and basic sportive skills (jumping, throwing, holding, balance…) and the development of lower and upper extremity trunk muscles of individuals with postural disorders.Our recreation programs include cycling, basketball, and soccer classes, offering a fun and engaging way to stay active and socialize.

- Basic English Speaking, Writing, and Math Tutoring

Communication and basic academic skills are vital in today’s world. Our tutoring sessions help individuals improve their English language proficiency and math skills, building a strong foundation for future success. With Communicative Translator/Provider technique that involves a person with a disability pointing to letters from multiple choice options with the aid of sensory “prompts” which are intended to maintain attention on the task and extinguish sensory-motor preoccupations.

- Yoga Fitness and Social Interaction Classes

Yoga is a holistic approach to health that focuses on fitness, mindfulness, and social interaction. Our yoga sessions foster a balanced and healthy lifestyle.
